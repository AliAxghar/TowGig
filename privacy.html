Why user background location is needed: TowGig Technologies, is an ondemand service providing platform that is mainly running on location based service providing system. The platform is built to accommodate 2 types of parties. The buyer who's seeking service, and expert, who's providing services. The job assignment algorithm is based upon distance between the buyer and expert. Hence, the background location feature is needed, because when a buyer request a job, the algorithm calculate the distance between the buyer seeking service and alert all the service providers within a given radius. When our algorithm hit the experts current location it then return to our system(server) to calculate the distance between the buyer and expert on the basis of live locations between both users. The system then decide what expert to assign the job to. The expert after accepting the job, move to the buyer's exact location to perform the task. For this entire process to be accurate and effective both users are recommended to allow the background location feature in the app for better delivery experience. Therefore, without the background location system, TowGig app, is completely redundant.
