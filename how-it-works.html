A guide on how to use TowGig.

To save time, money, and enjoy convenience on vehicle services. A few taps on your phone brings you auto smills. View tips below.

A Buyer

A buyer is anyone who buy the services of an auto service provider. Download the TowGig app & sign up for free now.

To place your first order, login to your account and select the service you need. Then proceed to customize it as needed under the following; Tow/Ship, Autocare & Roadside Help

After customizing the service, Choose payment method; & order Gig. Payment is done right in the app after the service is completed

When you request a Gig, an Expert will accept & you both connect to each other. You'll see Expert on the app in real time coming for your rescue.

Buyer & Expert can text or call each other with any question or concern done through the App.

Buyer can schedule a gig for a later time. You'll be notify when an Expert except your scheduled Gig request. Then relax & let TowGig technology handle the rest.

To increase savings on routine auto care, visit TowGig benefits, in your account dashboard for amazing deals awaiting you & your family/friends.

Certified Expert

An Expert is the one with auto service skills and ready to help a Buyer in need of car services

Download your free App, create account, fill and submit forms; we run a full screening checks to make sure you meet the Expert qualification standard 

If you qualify, you will be notify, and given access into the platform to start accepting service orders

If you've workers, they will continue doing works for you as usual. Though only 1 person for now. But we're setting up the system to support multiples employees in future .

Order alert will show service details of the type of Gig ordered. It will also show: Accept on your phone. Tap on "Accept", then head to the Buyer's for work. Call or Text the Buyer with any questions or concern before leaving to the service location.

Call/text the Buyer from the App in case of a question locating the Buyer or stock in traffic.

Get paid in 10 day for any amazing job done. Refer friends and families to earn $100 - $1000 bonus. Other benefits available for you inside also.

Frequently ask Questions

For Payments

When will I be paid?

You will be paid within 10 days for every gig that you completed.

What is the pay period?

The period is on the 10 day after the day the service was rendered.

How will I be paid?

You will be paid through direct deposit.

How can I check my pay?

You can check the summary of your pay in the TowGig app. You can update your account also any time.

Is there cash handling involved?

The only cash handling that will ever be involved is if a Buyer decides to tip you in cash.If not, we don't support cash handling.

What if I need to change my direct deposit?

You can update your bank account information and direct deposit details in the your TowGig Expert dashboard.

Employer/Employee

What if I have an Employee?

As a contractor with an employee they can work using your Ipad or Tablet device, to continue doing their amazing work. For now, only 1 electronic device can be use. We are about to power the system to accommodate multiple employees at a time. Which means, more jobs, revenue, and high growth. 

We only accept a certain number of service providers per region/county. So, sign up now & take your space before is late.
